About the studio

A family of specialists, spread across the map.

At Khatib Designs we design and build mobile & web apps — iOS, Android and web — for clients across the GCC and around the world. Our team is formed by specialists from all over, yet we work as one family, proud to deliver world-class quality wherever a project lives.

Our story started in 2015, when the founders opened their own design and development company — one that keeps work values high and delivers exclusive, considered products to every client.
