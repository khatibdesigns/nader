Support

We’re here to help you take great care of your pets. Most answers are below — and you can always email us.

Getting started

How do I add a pet?

Open Profile → Add another pet (or the + button), enter your pet’s name, species, breed and birthday, and optionally add a photo from your camera or gallery.

How do I add a vaccination or weight record?

Open your pet → Health tab → Record. Choose the type (vaccination, weight, vet visit or medication), add a title and date, and save.

How do reminders work?

When you add a calendar appointment you can choose a reminder (1 hour, 1 day or 1 week before). Enable Push reminders in Profile → Preferences and allow notifications when asked.

Vets & appointments

How do I call a vet or get directions?

On the Vets screen, tap Call, Message or Route on any saved clinic. Route opens Google Maps at the clinic’s address.

Can I add a time to an appointment?

Yes — when adding a calendar event you can set both the date and the time, plus the vet and a reminder.

Account & data

Is my data private?

Yes. Your pets, records and documents are stored in your own private account and are never shared with other users. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I switch language or theme?

Profile → Preferences. eVet supports English and Arabic (right-to-left) and System/Light/Dark appearance.
