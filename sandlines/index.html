What is Sandlines?

Sandlines is a faceless short-video channel that turns the most fascinating, lesser-known facts about the Arabian Peninsula and the Gulf — hidden history, staggering numbers, megaprojects, nature and wonders — into fast, beautifully produced vertical reels. Every video is researched, scripted, narrated, and human-reviewed before it goes live.

Daily reels

A fresh, surprising story about Arabia and the Gulf, published every day.

Bilingual

Arabic and English, made for audiences across the GCC and beyond.

Human-reviewed

Every reel is reviewed and approved by the creator before posting.
