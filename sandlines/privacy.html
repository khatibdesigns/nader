Sandlines — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Sandlines ("we", "the app") is a personal publishing tool operated by Khatib Designs that lets the owner of the TikTok account @sandlines.tv generate short educational videos and publish them to that same account. This policy explains what data we handle and why.

Who this app is for

Sandlines is a single-creator tool. It is used only by the channel owner to post their own content. It is not a multi-user product and does not onboard, store, or process data belonging to other people or accounts.

What we collect

When the creator authorizes Sandlines through Login with TikTok, TikTok provides us with:

  • Basic account information (TikTok open_id and display name) — to confirm videos publish to the correct profile.
  • An OAuth access token and refresh token — used solely to upload and publish the creator's own approved videos via the TikTok Content Posting API.

We do not collect personal data from viewers or any third parties, and we do not use TikTok login for advertising or profiling.

How we use it

Tokens and account identifiers are used only to publish the creator's reviewed videos to their TikTok profile. Each video is human-reviewed and approved before posting. AI-generated visuals or narration are disclosed to TikTok via the is_ai_generated flag where applicable.

Storage & security

Tokens are stored privately on the creator's own server (an access-controlled environment file) and are never committed to source control, shared, sold, or transferred to third parties. They are transmitted only to TikTok's official API over HTTPS.

Retention & revocation

Tokens are retained only while the integration is in use. The creator can revoke Sandlines' access at any time from TikTok Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions, which immediately invalidates the stored tokens.

Third parties

We share no data with third parties. The only external service contacted with this data is TikTok's own API.
