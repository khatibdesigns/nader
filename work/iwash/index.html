iWASH

Book a wash, get on shining.

iWASH brought Kuwait’s car-wash market on-demand. Drivers browse the washes available nearby, compare ratings and prices, pick an open time slot and book — then track the job all the way to a spotless finish, right from their phone.

The challenge

A real-time booking marketplace needs accurate availability across many vendors, transparent pricing, and a checkout locals actually trust — in Kuwait that means both cash and KNET.

What we built

We designed and built native iOS and Android apps with real-time slot booking, ratings & reviews, an in-app coupon wallet and KNET / cash checkout — wrapped in a clean, fast interface.

Key features

  • Real-time slot booking
  • Ratings & reviews
  • Cash / KNET checkout
  • In-app coupon wallet

The outcome

iWASH became one of Kuwait’s most popular delivery car-wash platforms.
