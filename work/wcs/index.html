WCS

Run the factory floor.

WCS is a management system for major steel factories across the USA. Supervisors view timesheets and wages, manage employees and projects, track budgets and live-chat with operation managers right on site — keeping every shift in sync.

The challenge

Enterprise operations are unforgiving: data has to be accurate, role-based and usable on a noisy factory floor. The app needed to consolidate timesheets, projects and budgets into something a supervisor could act on in seconds.

What we built

We designed and built native iOS and Android apps with timesheets & wages, project and task tracking, budget reporting and on-site live chat between supervisors and operation managers.

Key features

  • Timesheets & wages
  • Project & task tracking
  • On-site live chat
  • Budget reporting

The outcome

WCS shipped to iOS and Android, running operations for major US steel factories.
