Common questions

My run ended — can I keep going?

Yes. Right after a crash you can Revive once per run for 10 cores and keep flying. (Daily Rift runs can't be revived — same rules for everyone.)

I bought cores / Remove Ads but don't see it.

Make sure you're signed into the same Apple or Google account you purchased with, then tap Restore Purchases in the Hangar or in System settings. If it still doesn't appear, email us with your receipt.

How do I unlock new ships?

Open the Hangar and spend credits (earned by flying) or cores on EMBER, GHOST, or VOID — each has its own perk.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to System → Reset progress. This clears all local data on your device.

Is VEKTOR free?

Yes. VEKTOR is free to play. Optional in-app purchases (core packs, a pilot starter bundle, and Remove Ads) are available but never required.
