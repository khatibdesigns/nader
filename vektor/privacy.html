VEKTOR — Privacy Policy

KHD Game Studio · a Khatib Designs studio · Last updated July 2026

The short version

VEKTOR is an arcade flight game made for everyone. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. Your game progress is stored only on your device.

What stays on your device

Your best scores, credits, cores, daily-run streaks, settings, and unlocked ships are saved locally on your phone (device storage). They are not uploaded to us. Deleting the app removes this data.

Purchases

In-app purchases (core packs, the pilot starter bundle, and Remove Ads) are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat, which handles the transaction and tells the app what you bought. We never see your card or payment details. See Apple's and Google's privacy policies for how they process payments.

Analytics & ads

VEKTOR may use basic, anonymous app analytics (e.g., crash and performance data) to keep the game stable. This data is aggregated and is not used to identify you. The optional "Remove Ads" purchase disables advertising.

Children

VEKTOR is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we don't collect personal data or require accounts, the app is suitable for all ages. Purchases are gated behind your device's standard App Store / Google Play parental controls.

Your choices

You can clear all local data anytime in System → Reset progress, or by deleting the app. You can manage or restore purchases through your Apple or Google account.
